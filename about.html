We are a team of data enthusiasts dedicated to exploring and analyzing the intricacies of data ingestion and processing. Through our project "Data Showdown," we aim to compare and contrast the performance of Apache NiFi and Google Big Query, two prominent tools in the realm of data management.

Our goal is to provide insights into the efficiency, scalability, and reliability of these platforms, helping businesses and organizations make informed decisions about their data processing strategies.

Join us on this journey as we delve into the world of data and uncover the secrets to successful data management!
